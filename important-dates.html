Conference Dates

  • June 26 - 30, 2023 – ACM/IEE JCDL 2023 conference

Submission Deadlines

All dates are Anywhere on Earth (AoE)

  • January 29, 2023 – Research paper submissions
  • February 12, 2023 – Late breaking results, preliminary works, datasets submissions
  • Mid-March, 2023 – Notification of acceptance
  • April 2, 2023 – Final camera-ready deadline for all submissions

For any questions about paper submissions you may contact the program chairs by email to jcdl2023@easychair.org.
